Thank you for participating in this research study. Your participation is extremely valuable. As a reminder, the purpose of this study is to determine which features of a smart phone application (called RECESS) are effective in improving daily physical activity and reducing sedentary behaviour. This information will be used to help us understand how we can increase physical activity and reduce sedentary behaviour, and if this can be achieved using different features of a smartphone application.

Please remember that all data was collected anonymously. We will share this information with the research community through seminars, conferences, presentations, and journal articles. If you are interested in receiving copies of journal articles, or if you have any questions or concerns, please contact me at the email address listed below. This study has been reviewed and received ethics clearance through a University of Waterloo Research Ethics Committee (ORE#22034).

DISLOSURE: In the study, you were paired with a ‘buddy’ for the purpose of mutual coaching towards your activity goals. In fact, this ‘buddy’ was not another participant. The coaching that you received was provided by a chatbot, with possible supplementation by a researcher. Prior research showed that pairing with another individual may increase your likelihood of changing behaviour. However, pairing individuals may be difficult or infeasible in research and in practice. Pairing you with a chatbot buddy helps us understand whether coaching in this form is effective and feasible. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me at ppoupart@uwaterloo.ca.
